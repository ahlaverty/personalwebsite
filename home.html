About Aubrey H. Laverty

Philanthropy

After receiving her degree at the University of Michigan, Aubrey moved to Oregon to serve as an AmeriCorps*VISTA member at the United Community Action Network. This began her dedication to community service.

Mercy Home for Boys and Girls

Mercy Home for Boys and Girls is a Catholic charity based in Chicago. It offers kids a real home. A place where they can grow into successful men and women who will one day create loving homes for their own families.

Aubrey served as an Associate Board member advocating and attending events as a representative for the charity. She was on the "Have Mercy!" Gala committeee that planned Mercy Home's biggest fundraiser of the year.

University of Michigan Alumni Club of Greater Washington D.C. (UMDC)

UMDC is a 501(c)(3) and provides a $40,000 need-based scholarship to a D.C. area high school senior to assist with tuition for the University of Michigan.

While living in the D.C. area, Aubrey volunteer to serve on the Communications Committee and the Community Service Committee. She was also Communications Committee Co-chair for part of her volunteer time.

Aubrey was awarded both the "Rookie of the Year" and "Volunteer of the Quarter" for her hard work and dedication while volunteering with the club.
